Persimmon Cookies

Ingredients

  • Persimmon pulp
  • Baking soda
  • Flour
  • Cinnamon
  • Cloves
  • Nutmeg
  • Pinch of salt
  • Cup of Sugar
  • Stick of Butter
  • An Egg

Directions

  1. Place persimmon pulp in a small bowl. Stir in baking soda.
  2. Sift flour, spices, and salt in a separate bowl.
  3. In yet another bowl, beat the sugar and butter. Beat the egg in the persimmon mix
  4. Stir flour until just-combined. Flod in the mix-ins.
  5. Drop spoonfuls f dough onto prepared baking sheet.
  6. Bake until edges are golden. Cool on wire rack.

Your mileage may vary, since the recipe gave little info on quantities.
